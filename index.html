Chicken Soup with Egg-Yoghurt-Lemon Sauce Recipe

"Especially enjoyable during the winter months, this soup is rich in vitamins and minerals and can be enjoyed all year round."

Ingredients:

  • 1 chicken thigh with bone
  • 7 cups of water
  • 3 tablespoons of flour
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 heaping bowl of yoghurt
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2-3 drops of lemon juice

Method/Preparation

  1. Cook the chicken: Boil the chicken thigh in 7 cups of water until tender. Remove the chicken and shred the meat, then set aside.
  2. Prepare the roux: In a separate bowl, mix 3 tablespoons of flour with a little water to make a smooth paste.
  3. Mix the yogurt and eggs: In a large bowl, whisk together 1 heaping bowl of yogurt and 3 egg yolks until smooth.
  4. Temper the mixture: Gradually add a few ladlefuls of the hot chicken broth into the yogurt-egg mixture while stirring constantly. This prevents curdling.
  5. Combine: Pour the tempered yogurt mixture back into the pot with the remaining chicken broth. Stir gently over low heat, but do not boil.
  6. Add chicken and seasoning: Add the shredded chicken to the soup. Mince 3 cloves of garlic and add them to the soup. Season with 2–3 drops of lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve: Once heated through, pour into bowls and optionally drizzle with melted butter or sprinkle some red pepper flakes and dried mint. Serve warm.

Bon appétit!
